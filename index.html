Health Tips

How to Take Medicines Properly

Always take medicines as prescribed by your doctor. Some should be taken with food while others should be taken on an empty stomach.

Medicine Storage Tips

Store medicines in a cool, dry place away from sunlight. Keep them out of reach of children and check expiration dates regularly.

Understanding Prescriptions

Always read the medicine label carefully. If you have any questions about dosage or side effects, consult your pharmacist or doctor.
